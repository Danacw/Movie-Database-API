Comparisons: Budget vs. Performance

To complete this analysis, data was first extracted from The Movie Database API to create CSV files of films released from 2015-2019. The data was then grouped by genre to assess the distribution of films across our dataset.

Since adventure, action, family, and science fiction films were the highest distributed genres in our dataset, I chose to focus in on these four categories.

We found the most insight from the adventure genre, with the other three genres showing weaker relationships. However, this dataset was relatively small with only 99 films appended. With a larger dataset of films spanning 10 to 20 years, a more comprehensive analysis on the relationship between budget, revenue, and ratings could reveal stronger conclusions.
