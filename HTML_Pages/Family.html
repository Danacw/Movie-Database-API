Family Films Analysis

The family film genre provided little to no insight, where the relationship between budget and revenue was insignificant with an r-value of .01. The relationship between budget and IMDb ratings is slightly more apparent with an r-value of .40. However, this still suggests a weak relationship between the budget of family films and the IMDB ratings they receive. Since this analysis only covered 8 films in the family film category, a larger distribution could have provided more insight.
